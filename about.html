Story Behind Starting of COVID-19 Free Jobs Forum

Lay-offs, firings, furloughs dominate the headlines in May. COVID-19 has dramatically altered the hiring landscape in India. It was such news of the staggering number of layoffs and individual stories of loss, just a month after the lockdown in April, that prompted two entrepreneurs Suyash Jain & Rajat Agarwal to lay the foundation of COVID-19 Free Jobs Forum.

COVID-19 Free Jobs Forum is a social initiative (non-profit) to help people losing or not getting jobs during the Corona Pandemic. We are collecting necessary details from Job Seekers & sharing it with HR/Recruiters who are hiring currently. Over 500+ Recruiters are already participating and we will add more as we progress. To support Job Seekers in this situation we are curating multiple best resources & sharing it with them which can be helpful in upskill themselves.
